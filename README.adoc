== Usage

=== flat

Flats.

[source,bash]
----
$ seq 5 | flat
1 2 3 4 5

$ seq 5 | flat -n 2
1 2
3 4
5

$ seq 5 | flat -d ,
1,2,3,4,5
----

=== rep

Repeat words.

[source,bash]
----
$ rep 5 A
AAAAA

$ echo 'A B' | rep 5 -i
A BA BA BA BA B

$ rep 1 3 5 A
A
AAA
AAAAA

$ rep $(seq 5) A
A
AA
AAA
AAAA
AAAAA
----

=== ucut

Unicode cut.

[source,bash]
----
$ echo １あ２あ３ | ucut -d あ -f 1,2
１ ２
----

=== align

Align texts.

[source,bash]
----
% echo $'123\nあいう\nえお' | align right 
   123
あいう
  えお

% echo $'1234\nああああああ\nうえお' | align center -p =  
====1234====
ああああああ
===うえお===
----
